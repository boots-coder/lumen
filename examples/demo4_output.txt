  Demo 4: 多模型格式适配

  📐 同一个工具在不同模型下的 Schema 格式:

    gpt-4o                    → openai     type=function, function.name=bash  
    claude-sonnet-4-6         → anthropic  name=bash, has input_schema  tokens=None
    deepseek-chat             → deepseek   type=function, function.name=bash  tokens=<｜tool▁call▁begin｜>
    llama3.1                  → llama      type=function, function.name=bash  tokens=<|python_tag|>
